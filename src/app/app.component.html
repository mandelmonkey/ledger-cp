"I can't connect my ledger?"

Make sure your ledger is a nano s and is updated to the latest firmware and had the latest bitcoin app installed. if the problem persists check your cable or try another one and refresh the browser.

"Are my tokens 100% secure now?"

Even though the transaction is signed on the ledger it is constructed via indiesquare api, therefore in the unlikley event indiesquare api is comprimsed you may be tricked into signing a fake transaction. Currently the ledger will confirm the btc outputs on the device but not the counterparty token transaction. To mitigate this you are able to view the raw unsiged tx before signing on the webapp and thus you can manually decode this to verify the data, however this will not help if your client system is also comprimised. There will soon be developed a native ledger counterparty app which will allow you to verify the coutnerparty transaction data aswell securley on the ledger device.
